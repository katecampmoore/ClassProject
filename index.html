Dear Friend

Thank you for taking the time to consider us as your child’s adoptive parents! You inspire us with your strength and dignity as you make this important decision for you and your child’s life. We know how hard of a decision this is for you and we want you to know how much we already love you and your child. Garrett and I have dreamed of adoption since before we were married, and are committed to providing a safe and loving home while providing educational and adventurous upbringing.

Our Adoption Pledge

We vow to guide and protect our children while also allowing them opportunities to be independent. We pledge to show love and respect to our children, and we promise to model a positive self image and compassion towards others while empowering children to make a positive difference in the world. We promise to love unconditionally despite our imperfections, and we promise to be show love to each other despite our faults. We promise to raise our child knowing that you placed him or her for adoption out of love, and always refer to you with respect and honor.
